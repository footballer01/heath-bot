ABOUT US




GG International School, established in 2007, is a top-ranked CBSE school in Pune, India. It is NABET accredited and ISO certified, offering co-educational education from preschool to senior secondary levels1. The school has branches in Pimpri, Bavdhan, and an upcoming branch in Tathawade. Known for its excellent academic record, 100% results in class 10th and 12th for 12 consecutive years, GGIS also excels in sports, producing champions in football, taekwondo, basketball, swimming, and athletics1. The school emphasizes holistic development, integrating life skills, Sanskaars, sports education, and more into its curriculum. State-of-the-art facilities include interactive classrooms, computer labs, robotics labs, and sports facilities2. GGIS is committed to providing high-quality education and empowering cultural growth. The school is part of the Goel Ganga Foundation, which aims to contribute meaningfully to society1. With a strong focus on both academics and extracurricular activities, GGIS prepares students to make responsible choices and excel in various fields.
